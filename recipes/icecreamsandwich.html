Homemade Ice Cream Sandwich

What is a Homemade Ice Cream Sandwich?

Food from my childhood!

Want ice cream but with a twist? This is one way to do it!

All you need is a pair of white sliced bread, and any ice cream will do!

Let's make a Homemade Ice Cream Sandwich!

The Ingredients

  • Two Pieces of White Sliced Bread (Although any other bread will do!)
  • Ice Cream (Preferrably Chocolate or Vanilla!)

The Directions

  1. Toast and prepare your sliced bread.
  2. Scoop One Whole Cup of your choice of Ice Cream, and place it between your sliced bread.
  3. Put the Sliced Bread together, forming a sandwich. Enjoy!
